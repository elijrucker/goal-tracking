## Goal Description

Work through The Art of Computer Programming (TAOCP) by Donald Knuth. Arguably the most comprehensive and rigorous work in all of computer science. Bill Gates famously said "If you think you're a really good programmer... read Art of Computer Programming." A multi-volume, decades-long work that represents the pinnacle of CS theory. The ultimate achievement in the Spades suit. Approach with patience - completing meaningful portions is the goal, not necessarily all volumes cover-to-cover.
