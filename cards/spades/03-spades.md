## Reflection & Lessons Learned

- **What surprised you?** First half was harder than expected — Strategy, Data, and Algorithms were new territory despite prior applied experience.
- **What would you do differently?** Read in subsection chunks from the start rather than attempting full chapters.
- **One thing that worked well:** Staggered sequencing with CS50x — concepts hit twice with enough gap to solidify.
- **One thing harder than expected:** The theory-heavy chapters in the first half; background was more applied than foundational.
- **Patterns noticed:** Spades cards benefit from staggered overlap rather than parallel or fully sequential reading.
- **Insights for similar future work:** CS Distilled functions best as a conceptual primer before denser Spades cards (8♠️ OS, 9♠️ CS:APP). Space them close but not simultaneous.
