## Goal Description

Read and work through Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs (SICP) by Abelson and Sussman. One of the most influential CS texts ever written. Teaches fundamental programming concepts through Scheme - abstraction, recursion, interpreters, and the nature of computation itself. Mind-expanding and challenging. A transformative read for any serious programmer.
