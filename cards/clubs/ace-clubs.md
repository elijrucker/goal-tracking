Make a meaningful contribution to an open source project. This is the ultimate applied practice achievement - demonstrating the ability to navigate real-world codebases, collaborate with a community, meet professional code quality standards, and contribute something genuinely valuable. Goes beyond personal projects into real-world collaborative development.
