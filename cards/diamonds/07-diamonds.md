## Goal Description

Develop proficiency across three CI/CD tools used in professional development workflows: Bitbucket (version control and pipelines), Bamboo (build and deployment automation), and Octopus Deploy (deployment automation). These tools are closely related and will largely be learned together as part of a professional deployment pipeline.
