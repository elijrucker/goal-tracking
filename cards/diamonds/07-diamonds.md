## Goal Description

Develop proficiency across three CI/CD tools used in professional development workflows: Bitbucket (version control and pipelines), Bamboo (build and deployment automation), and Octopus Deploy (deployment automation). These tools are closely related and will largely be learned together as part of a professional deployment pipeline.

Learn and master the CI/CD toolchain ecosystem: Bitbucket (version control), Bamboo (continuous integration), and Octopus Deploy (continuous deployment). Additionally, integrate BMAD (Breakthrough Method for Agile AI-Driven Development) as a modern workflow orchestration tool that assigns specialized AI personas (Product Manager, Architect, Developer, QA) to collaborate on structured, enterprise-grade software engineering. BMAD replaces unpredictable "vibe coding" with deterministic, step-by-step execution.
