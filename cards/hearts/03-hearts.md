## Goal Description

Develop understanding of global financial markets and macroeconomics. Provides the broader economic context needed for international investing across Eastern Europe, Southeast Asia, and South America. Yale's Financial Markets course by Robert Shiller is the primary resource - macro concepts are universal and apply regardless of investing location.
