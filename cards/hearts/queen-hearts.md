## Goal Description

Achieve Polish C1 (Advanced/Proficient) certification on the CEFR scale. C1 represents the ability to express ideas fluently, use language flexibly, and understand complex texts. A long-term commitment requiring consistent study and immersion. Directly enables EU relocation to Poland or a Polish-speaking community, strengthens the Eastern European investment strategy, and represents a significant personal achievement.
