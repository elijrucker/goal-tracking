## Goal Description

Study growth investing strategy - identifying and analyzing high-growth companies and sectors. Complements the index investing foundation with a more active approach focused on growth potential. Peter Lynch and Philip Fisher are the primary resources. Principles apply globally and across markets including European and emerging market investments.

---

## Resources

- One Up On Wall Street by Peter Lynch (book)
- Common Stocks and Uncommon Profits by Philip Fisher (book)
- The Little Book That Builds Wealth by Pat Dorsey (supplementary)
