## Resources

**Primary:**

- The Little Book of Common Sense Investing by John Bogle (book) — foundational index investing philosophy
- [Bogleheads Forum](https://www.bogleheads.org) - Index investing community
- [European Bogleheads Wiki](https://www.bogleheads.org/wiki/Investing_from_Europe) - EU-specific guidance

**Fundamental & Value Analysis:**

- [Damodaran - Foundations of Finance](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/webcastfoundationsonline.htm) - NYU Stern, free — ~3-4 hrs; most-cited equity valuation academic
- [Damodaran - Investment Philosophies](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/) - NYU Stern, free — empirical evidence on whether active strategies work; Bogleheads-compatible framing

**Technical Analysis:**

- [IG Academy - Technical Analysis](https://www.ig.com/en/learn-trading/ig-academy) - FCA-regulated broker, free — ~4-5 hrs; broker-neutral pedagogy

**Options:**

- [Options Industry Council](https://www.optionseducation.org) - OIC, free — income strategy track ~5-10 hrs; Schwab itself licenses OIC content

**Futures:**

- [CME Institute - Introduction to Futures](https://www.cmegroup.com/education.html) - CME Group, free — ~3-6 hrs; published by world's largest futures exchange

**Forex:**

- [BabyPips - School of Pipsology](https://www.babypips.com/learn/forex) - free — ~6-8 hrs core; 20-year industry standard forex education resource

**Global Markets Context:**

- [Yale Financial Markets - Robert Shiller](https://www.coursera.org/learn/financial-markets-global) - Coursera, free to audit — macro concepts universal across markets
