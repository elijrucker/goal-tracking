## Goal Description

First milestone on the 75 lb total weight loss journey. Losing the first 25 lbs establishes habits, builds momentum, and demonstrates that the system is working. The first milestone is often the most important psychologically - it proves the goal is achievable.

**Dietary Approach:** Calorie deficit combined with intermittent fasting and whole foods focus:

- Begin fasting around 6pm, 5 nights per week (proven effective from previous weight loss success)
- Restrict meat consumption (plant-based emphasis)
- Eliminate heavily processed foods (focus on whole, minimally processed ingredients)
- This approach provides structure, improves food quality, and maintains flexibility

## Reflection & Lessons Learned

- **What surprised you?**: Weight loss cycles include brief gains even during an overall downward trend — confirmed pattern from earlier observation.
- **What would you do differently?**: Start daily weigh-ins earlier when within ~1 lb of a milestone, rather than after the first crossing.
- **One thing that worked well**: Requiring a 2-week maintenance window (not just a single crossing) before closing the card — prevented premature closure on May 20.
- **One thing harder than expected**: Distinguishing "noise" (288.2 on Jun 7) from a genuine plateau in real time.
- **Patterns noticed**: Plateau/rebound cycles roughly every 2-4 weeks. Elliptical resistance level increases (L10 → L18) tracked alongside weight loss without pace degradation.
- **Insights for similar future work**: For 7♥️ (50 lb / 261.8 lb target), apply the same daily-weigh-in protocol once within ~1 lb of threshold, and expect a similar rebound pattern near the close.
